Chili

Description

This is the best chili recipe I have ever tried. I'm not sure where the recipe originated, but it is amazing! Sometimes, I don't bother adding all four cans of the kidney beans, but I like my chili thick and meaty.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can of stewed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can of tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can of kidney beans with liquid
  • 1 (15 ounce) can of pinto beans with liquid
  • 1 ½ cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 dash of ground black pepper
  • 1 dash of hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHot®), or to taste

Steps

  1. Cook and stir ground beef and onion in a large skillet until meat is brown and onions are tender, about 10 minutes.
  2. Transfer beef and onion mixture to a slow cooker; stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, kidney beans, pinto beans, and water.
  3. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and hot pepper sauce.
  4. Cover and cook on Low for 8 hours.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
